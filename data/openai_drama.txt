Sam Altman was abruptly fired as CEO of OpenAI by the board of directors in 
November 2023. The board cited a lack of transparency in their decision. 
The firing shocked the technology industry and led to widespread uncertainty 
about the future of OpenAI.

Greg Brockman, who was President of OpenAI, resigned in protest immediately 
after Sam Altman was fired. A large majority of OpenAI employees threatened 
to resign and join Microsoft if the board did not reinstate Sam Altman.

Sam Altman was reinstated as CEO of OpenAI just five days after being fired. 
A new board was formed following the crisis. Microsoft, which had invested 
heavily in OpenAI, played a significant role in the resolution of the conflict.
