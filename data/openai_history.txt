OpenAI was founded in December 2015 by Sam Altman, Elon Musk, Greg Brockman, 
Ilya Sutskever, and Wojciech Zaremba. The organization is headquartered in 
San Francisco, California. OpenAI started as a non-profit research laboratory 
with the mission to develop safe artificial intelligence.

Elon Musk resigned from the OpenAI board in February 2018, citing potential 
conflicts of interest with his work at Tesla. Despite his departure from the 
board, OpenAI continued to grow rapidly.

In 2019, OpenAI transitioned to a capped-profit model and received a one billion 
dollar investment from Microsoft. This partnership allowed OpenAI to access 
significant computing resources through Microsoft Azure.
